Perform secure message signing / verification!

Uses:

  • Enter a Password to get your unique ID!
  • Enter a Password+Message, to get an ID+Signature verifying the Message was sent by you.
  • Enter a Message+ID+Signature to verify if the Signature matches the Message+ID of the sender.

Unless they know your password, no one can produce a correct signature for a message from your ID.

Everything uses secure client-side javascript, so no information leaves your device.

This website uses the Elliptic Curve Digital Signature Algorithm with 512-bit security and SHA-256 hashing. ID's and Signatures are provided and accepted in a custom base64. ID QR codes can be scanned to get the base64 ID.
