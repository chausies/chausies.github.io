# **Practice Sight-reading with Mic Support**

Sight-reading practice webapp that supports keyboard typing input **and**
listening to real instruments or voice through your mic. Completely open
source and runs locally in your browser. Check it out at
[chausies.xyz/sightreading](https://chausies.xyz/sightreading).

## **Overview**

The webapp generates an infinite stream of notes on a virtual staff. Users
read the notes and input them (via typing or mic+instrument/voice) to
advance.  The app tracks accuracy and net speed for the session.

## **Features**

* **Infinite Stream of Notes:** Endless practice stream that dynamically
  generates notes in various modes, including from chords and random.  
* **Supports Clefs and Keys:** Use any clef (including both bass and treble 
  aka grand staff) with any major/minor key, as well as 8va/8vb
  transpositions.  
* **Microphone mode:** Listens on your mic for an instrument
  being played or your voice being used. Great for use with a smart
  phone/tablet\!  
* **View Stats:** Check your accuracy and penalized notes per minute
  (NPM).  
* **Teaching Mode:** Starts you off with a few notes, and dynamically 
  introduces new notes/bring backs old notes based on your performance.

*Note: This project was largely vibecoded using Gemini.*
